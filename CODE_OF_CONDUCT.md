# Community code of conduct

FridaBox is a technical research project. Participation must remain respectful,
evidence-driven, and safe.

## Expected behavior

- Treat contributors, maintainers, upstream projects, and users with respect.
- Critique technical claims with reproducible evidence rather than personal
  attacks.
- Respect authorization boundaries, privacy, licenses, and coordinated security
  disclosure.
- Make room for different experience levels while maintaining engineering rigor.
- Clearly distinguish measured results from assumptions or speculation.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, publication of secrets or private
data, knowingly false claims, and requests for unauthorized exploitation are not
welcome. Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions that violate
these expectations and may restrict participation when necessary.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mahdikarzari),
[Telegram](https://t.me/QM4RS), or GitHub security-advisory channel. Good-faith
reports will be handled with discretion.
